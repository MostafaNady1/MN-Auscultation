Privacy Policy for MN Auscultation

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

MN Auscultation is an offline educational mobile application designed for learning cardiac and chest auscultation sounds. The app provides medical educational content including audio recordings, images, and textual explanations of clinical findings.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

2. Information We Collect

MN Auscultation does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

  • No account creation is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No medical or sensitive data is collected
  • No user data is stored on external servers

3. Offline Functionality

The app is primarily fully functional offline. All educational content (sounds, images, and text) is stored locally on the user’s device.

Internet access is only used for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Loading rewarded ads to unlock optional content

4. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including rewarded ads.

AdMob may collect and process certain non-personal data such as:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (such as model, OS version)
  • General app usage data for ad performance

This data is collected and processed by Google under its own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

The developer does not have access to or control over this data.

5. Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • External storage access

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data.

Third-party services (such as Google AdMob) may independently collect limited data as described above.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for medical education purposes. It does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age.

9. Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored by the app, risk of data exposure is minimal. All educational content is stored locally on the device.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
