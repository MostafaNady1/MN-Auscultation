Privacy Policy for MN Auscultation

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

MN Auscultation is an offline educational mobile application designed for learning cardiac and chest auscultation sounds. The app provides medical educational content including audio recordings, images, and textual explanations of clinical findings.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

2. Information We Collect

The app itself does not directly collect or store personal data from users.

  • No account creation is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No medical or sensitive data is collected by the developer

However, third-party advertising services used in the app may collect certain data automatically (see Section 4).

3. Offline Functionality

The app is primarily functional offline. All educational content (sounds, images, and text) is stored locally on the user’s device.

Internet access is only used for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Loading rewarded ads to unlock optional content

4. Advertisements (AdMob & Mediation Partners)

This app uses Google AdMob with mediation, which may include third-party ad networks such as Unity Ads.

These advertising partners may collect and process certain data automatically, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • IP address (may be used for general location estimation)
  • App interaction and ad performance data

This data is used to:

  • Serve relevant ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob:
    https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Ads:
    https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

The developer does not have direct access to this data.

Consent: Where required (such as in the European Economic Area), users may be asked to provide consent for personalized or non-personalized ads.

5. Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Precise location
  • External storage access

6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell or share user data.

However, third-party advertising partners (such as AdMob and Unity Ads) may process data independently as described above.

7. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Unity Ads (advertising via mediation)

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for educational purposes. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children. However, third-party ad providers may collect data as described in this policy.

9. Security

Since the app does not directly store personal data, risks are minimal. All educational content is stored locally on the device.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date.
